In this first session you will learn:

  • Myths and facts about children with disabilities
  • Guiding principles to support children with disabilities
  • Why children with disabilities are at a greater risk of abuse and neglect
  • Why it's more difficult to identify abuse and neglect in children with disabilities
  • Appropriate language to use when speaking to and about children with disabilities
